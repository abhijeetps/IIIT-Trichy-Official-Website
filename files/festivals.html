Festivals

Festivals create the most extraordinary memories for students during there college life.

IIIT Trichy knows this, and thus has created a perfect enviroment where students can celebrate Festivals with full fun.

Institute Day

The Institute Day is celebrated every year at the end of even semester. On this day, meritorius students are awareded Certificates and Medals. Many cultural events are performed by the students.
In 2017, the event was held on 7th April, 2017. On this day, many meritorius students were awarded Certificates and Medals. The Chief Guest for the event was Shri. Paul Pandian, Group Director, ISRO Inertial Systems, Thiruvanathpuram.

Sports Day

The Sports Day is celebrated every year at the beginning of the odd semester. The event comprises of many popular sports like Cricket, Badminton, Kabaddi, Chess, Basketball, Football and Table Tennis. The winners are awarded trophies and medals.
